Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum located in Agra, India. It is considered to be the greatest architectural achievement in the whole indo-islamic architecture. It's recognised architectonic beauty has a rhytmic combinationof solid and voids, concave and convex and light shadows, such as arches and domes further increases the aesthetic aspect. Not a piece of architecture, as other buildings are the proud passions of an emperor's love wrought in living stones.
